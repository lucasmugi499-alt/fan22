# GoalPlace256 Sponsor Package

## Sponsor Positioning

GoalPlace256 sponsorship helps local athletes, teams, leagues, women and youth programs, and annual recognition moments grow with transparent reporting.

The sponsor model is built around sporting integrity:

> League standings are based only on match results. Paid tools never affect sporting rankings.

## Package Options

### Athlete Supporter

Starting from: 500k UGX

Best for: Local businesses that want direct athlete impact.

Includes:

- Sponsored athlete profile placement
- Monthly support summary
- Verified support moments in the impact feed
- Basic photo and story reporting

### Team Partner

Starting from: 2M UGX

Best for: Brands supporting equipment, travel, matchday needs, or youth team operations.

Includes:

- Team page recognition
- Team support reporting
- Matchday story placement
- Quarterly impact summary

### League Builder

Starting from: 6M UGX

Best for: Partners helping leagues publish fixtures, verify results, improve media quality, and strengthen admin operations.

Includes:

- League page recognition
- GoalPlace Index reporting
- Fixture and media reporting
- Verified league operations summary

### Annual Awards Sponsor

Starting from: Custom

Best for: Organizations supporting credible recognition across athletes, teams, leagues, and community contributors.

Includes:

- Awards category presence
- Finalist story package
- Recognition event support
- Annual impact report

### Women And Youth Sport Sponsor

Starting from: Custom

Best for: Partners funding targeted development programs with measurable community value.

Includes:

- Program page recognition
- Athlete and team support reporting
- Youth participation summary
- Community impact story package

## Sponsor Reporting

Every sponsor package can include:

- Funds allocated by athlete, team, league, or program
- Verified support moments
- Media uploads and story links
- Fan engagement summary
- Admin reliability and reporting cadence
- GoalPlace Index snapshot for league programs

## Boundaries

Sponsors can improve visibility, reporting, community support, and operational resources.

Sponsors cannot influence:

- Match results
- League standings
- Official awards eligibility
- Athlete verification decisions
- Result verification decisions
- Admin dispute outcomes

## Recommended Pilot Bundle

For the first 90 days, package the pilot as:

- 1 League Builder partner
- 2 Team Partners
- 5 Athlete Supporters
- 1 Women And Youth Sport Sponsor

This creates enough support activity and reporting data to validate sponsor value without compromising sporting rankings.
