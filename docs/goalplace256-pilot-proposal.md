# GoalPlace256 Pilot Proposal

## Purpose

GoalPlace256 will run a focused pilot with selected Ugandan grassroots leagues to prove a mobile-first model for verified athlete support, league operations, community engagement, and sponsor reporting.

The pilot is designed around one non-negotiable rule:

> League standings are based only on match results. Paid tools never affect sporting rankings.

## Pilot Scope

Duration: 90 days

Recommended cohort:

- 3 leagues across football, basketball, and rugby
- 9 to 18 teams
- 75 to 150 athlete profiles
- 20 to 40 fixtures
- 3 to 6 sponsor or community partners

## League Status Model

Draft League: Created but not public.

Community League: Public league page with teams, fixtures, athletes, and posts. Support and payout tools are limited.

Verified League: Confirmed by GoalPlace256. Can run verified performance challenges, athlete payouts, official standings, and annual awards eligibility.

Partner League: Verified league with advanced tools, sponsor reporting, analytics, and priority support.

Suspended: League restricted due to fraud, disputes, or verification issues.

## Operating Model

GoalPlace256 provides:

- League profile setup and onboarding
- Team, fixture, athlete, and admin setup
- Verification workflow for results and performance records
- Athlete support tracking and payout review
- GoalPlace Index reporting
- Sponsor impact reporting
- Weekly pilot review and issue tracking

League admins provide:

- Fixture schedule and venues
- Team and athlete rosters
- Match result evidence
- Admin reviewers for verification queues
- Community updates and media uploads

## Standings And Index Separation

League Standings:

- Based only on match results
- Sorted from completed fixture scores
- Never affected by payment, package level, sponsor participation, or platform plan

GoalPlace Index:

- Based on platform quality signals
- Signals include verification, match completion rate, athlete profile completion, fan engagement, support activity, admin reliability, and media uploads
- Used for operational trust, sponsor reporting, and league readiness
- Never used to alter sporting standings

## Pilot Milestones

Week 1: League onboarding, admin setup, status assignment, and roster import.

Weeks 2-4: Public league pages, fixture publishing, athlete profile completion, and initial posts.

Weeks 5-8: Verified result workflow, support tracking, sponsor reporting, and GoalPlace Index snapshots.

Weeks 9-12: Awards eligibility review, pilot report, sponsor package renewal plan, and rollout recommendations.

## Success Metrics

- At least 80 percent athlete profile completion
- At least 85 percent fixture completion reporting
- Result verification completed within 48 hours
- Public pages active for every Community, Verified, or Partner League
- Sponsor reporting delivered monthly
- No evidence that paid tools influence sporting standings

## Deliverables

- Live league profile pages
- League admin dashboard workflow
- League status badges and verification model
- League standings table
- GoalPlace Index report
- Sponsor impact report
- Pilot closeout brief with expansion recommendation

## Decision Gate

At the end of the pilot, GoalPlace256 should decide which leagues remain Community League, which graduate to Verified League, and which are ready for Partner League support.
